Our Heritage
"A fragrance is an invisible garment — the most intimate thing you can wear."

Every bottle is a story waiting to become yours.

The Anatomy of Scent

Fragrance Notes

🌸
Top Notes

The First Impression

Bergamot, pink pepper, and citrus — the vivid opening that greets the world. Bold, bright, and fleeting. These notes evaporate within 15 minutes, making way for the heart.

🌹
Heart Notes

The Soul

Rose, jasmine, and spice — the true character of the fragrance. Emerging after 20 minutes and lasting for hours, the heart is where a perfume reveals itself completely.

🪵
Base Notes

The Memory

Oud, sandalwood, and musks — the lasting impression. Rich, deep, and anchoring, base notes linger on skin for hours after the top and heart have faded into their embrace.
